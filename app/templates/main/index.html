What's eFBi?

Easily exchange contact details with a simple tap! eFBi simplifies the way you share and manage your contact information using NFC technology. Whether you're networking at an event, meeting new people, or simply staying connected with friends, eFBi is the perfect tool for hassle-free contact sharing.

Manage Your Profile

Keep your contact information up-to-date within the app. Update your name, email, phone number, Whatsapp, Telegram, Instagram, Facebook and more with ease.

How To Setup Your Account?

Getting started with eFBi couldn't be simpler! Begin by ordering your personalized NFC tag. Once it arrives, simply scan the tag with your phone. You'll be seamlessly redirected to a personalized signup page on our web app. Complete the signup process, including confirming your email account. Then, effortlessly enter your contact details, such as your phone number, Facebook, LinkedIn, Instagram, and more. The next time you scan the NFC tag, your contact details will be displayed seamlessly. Enjoy the ease and convenience of eFBi! Let's simplify communication together.
